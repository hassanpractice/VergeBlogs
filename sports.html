Pakistan's Cricket Journey in 2025

Dec 12, 2025 | Ali Khan

The Pakistan cricket team has shown remarkable improvement throughout 2025, with notable performances in both Test and limited-overs formats. Under the new leadership, the team has developed a more aggressive and strategic approach to the game.

Key Achievements in 2025:

  • Series Victory: Won test series against top-ranked teams
  • Young Talent: Emergence of promising fast bowlers
  • Batting Consistency: Improved performance in pressure situations
  • T20 World Cup: Strong showing with semi-final appearance

Looking ahead to 2026, the team is focusing on building a balanced squad capable of competing in all conditions. The combination of experienced players and emerging young talent creates a promising future for Pakistan cricket in international tournaments.
